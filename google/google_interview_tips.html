Interviewing Tips

What are coding interviews?

Focus mainly on technical skills. 30~35 mins interactive question.

Have a good understanding of data structures and also the question.

It's OK that you may not have the best solution at the first time but communication is really important to come up with a better one.

What's the best advice to solve coding questions?

Try to think outloud.

First ask clarification questions. Then call out assumptions and explain your thoughts clearly before jump into coding.

Find the weird edge cases first. In this case, you are able to know what kind of inputs you are getting.

Speak outloud so that you mind gets through some sort of uncertainty and interviewers are able to give some hints.

What are the tips to prepare for an interview?

Not think about that it is an interview. Instead, think about how you deal with software engineering in general so do some practice first!

Google tech dev guide is a good point to start to make sure the fundamentals are stong.

Get used to coding on Whiteboard. You can take a piece of paper and code as well.

Other advice?

Don't need to be an expert of algorithms just be good at them.
